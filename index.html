Stellar age of M44

The Beehive cluster is one of the nearest open cluster in Cancer constellation. It is not far from us, so it is not hard to observe the luminosity and it is highly observable during February to May in my location(Vancouver!). I use several image processing technique and fitting isochrone to estimate the stellar age of this cluster.
