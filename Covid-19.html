Introduction

COVID-19 is a contagious diseas caused by severe acute respiratory syndrome coronavirus 2, which lead to the COVID-19 pandemic. Here, I will analysis COVID-19 data from 2019-2020 by using SIR model. Theory

COVID-19 is a major health issues around the world now. Here, I am using the confirmed, deaths and recoverd cases data from Jan/22 to Mar/30 (updated), to do a time series analysis in order to find the tendency of virus and what causes the infected number to go down. For the temporal behaviour of the outbreak, the model is SIR model. The model can be written as:

$$\frac{d \mathbf{S}}{d \mathbf{t}} = -\beta \frac{\mathbf{SI}}{S_{0}}$$ $$\frac{d \mathbf{I}}{d \mathbf{t}} = \beta \frac{\mathbf{SI}}{S_{0}} - \gamma I$$ $$\frac{d \mathbf{R}}{d \mathbf{t}} = \gamma I$$

Where S is the number of susceptible and satisfies: $$S = S_{0} - I - R$$

Beta is the rate at which infected people infect others. Gamma is the rate of recovery. By using data from Data.

Conclusion

From the change in infection, we can see if the susceptible population is high, the number of infection will have large increases from start. From "Nature news" and “Baidu news", China locked down Wuhan as soon as the infection number in Wuhan got large. Lockdown means less people contact which controls the value of Si for other provinces, and Wuhan Si would be decreasing and approach to zero. Also, China built a hospital in 10 days which means there were more place to isolate and recover infected people in a short time, cause inflection rate change to lower and recovery rate increases. Another important factor is mask, almost 95% of people in China worn mask during that time, which causes infected rate even lower.
